The United Kingdom

-The United Kingdom is a island nation in northwestern Europe

-It officially dates from 1707

-It comprises England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland, with a population of over 69 million in 2024

-Covers 94,354 square miles (244,376 km2)

-The capital and largest city of both England and the UK is London; Edinburgh, Cardiff and Belfast

-Ranks amongst the largest economies by nominal GDP and is one of the world's largest exporters and importers

-United Kingdom lies between the North Atlantic Ocean and the North Sea with the southeast
